Inkohliso yaseBerners Street yenziwa umlobi uTheodore Hook eWestminster (manje eyingxenye yeLondon) ngo-1810. Ngemva kwamasonto ambalwa ezilungiselela wenza ukubheja ngokuzenzakalelayo nomngane wakhe ukuthi angashintsha noma iyiphi indawo ibe ikheli okukhulunywa ngalo kakhulu eLondon. UHook wachitha amasonto ayisithupha ethumela izincwadi eziphakathi kwenkulungwane nezine kubahwebi namabhizinisi e-oda ukulethwa kwezimpahla zabo nezinkonzo ku-54 Berners Street, eWestminster, ngezikhathi ezihlukahlukene ngomhla ka-27 November 1810. Abantu abaningana abaziwayo nabo bamenywa ukuba bavakashele ikheli, kuhlanganise nosihlalo BeBhange LaseNgilandi kanye Nenkampani Yase-East India, uMbusi WaseLondon Mayorce kanye noMbusi waseGlou Mayorce. 

UHook nabangane bakhe baqashe amagumbi endlini ebhekene nenombolo 54 ukuze babuke okuqhubekayo. Abashanela bakashimula baqala ukufika ekhelini ngo-5:00 ekuseni ngalolo suku, balandelwa amakhulu abameleli bemisebenzi yezandla namabhizinisi amaningana, kuhlanganise nabadayisi, abangcwabi, abadayisa ukudla, abadayisi, ababhaki, abapheki bekhekhe nezingcweti zokudansa; ukulethwa kwezimpahla bekuhlanganisa izitho zomzimba, ifenisha, amalahle, amakhekhe omshado, ukudla, iziphuzo kanye nebhokisi. Amaphoyisa abizwe ukuthi azozama ukulawula isixuku kodwa awakwazanga ukususa umgwaqo kwaze kwaba ngemva kokutheleka kokugcina kwezivakashi ngo-5:00 ntambama: izisebenzi zasendlini ezazicabanga ukuthi zizohlolelwa umsebenzi. 

U-Hook wayengaziwa ngaleso sikhathi, kodwa wavuma ukuzibandakanya kwakhe kunoveli ye-semi-autobiographical eyanyatheliswa eminyakeni engamashumi amabili nanhlanu ngemva komcimbi. Umkhonyovu waphindwa kulo lonke elaseBrithani naseParis, waphinde waxoxwa esiteji, ngengoma nangabadwebi bamakhathuni.

Theodore Hook, umenzi wenkohliso, c. Ngo-1810 
uTheodore Hook wayengumbhali wama-comic opera kanye nama-farces esiteji kanye nombhali wamanoveli amaningana. Wayenohlangothi olubi emvelweni yakhe futhi wayaziwa njengomgiliqili nomkhohlisi.[1] Isazi-mlando uJudith Flanders siphawula ukuthi wayaziwa kakhulu ngamahlaya akhe awusizo njengoba ayaziwa ngokubhala kwakhe.[2] Ngesikhathi sokukhohlisa kukaBerners Street wayeneminyaka engama-22 ubudala. 

Ukuhlelwa kukaHook ngale nkohliso kwaqala ngo-October 1810, emasontweni ayisithupha ngaphambi komcimbi, futhi wayenabangane ababili ababezomsiza ngakho.[4] Akwaziwa ukuthi obani ababehambisana naye—owesilisa nowesifazane oyedwa—kodwa kucatshangwa ukuthi omunye wabo kamuva waba umlingisi odumile.[5] Inombolo enkulu yezincwadi yathunyelwa kubahwebi, abahlinzeki kanye nabaziwayo kusetshenziswa izaba ezahlukene; izilinganiso zenani lezinhlamvu ziyahlukahluka kusukela "okungenani kwenkulungwane"[6] kuya ezinkulungwaneni ezine.

Izaba ezisetshenzisiwe zashintsha kuye ngokuthi ubani owayethintwa, kodwa bonke babeneziqondiso ezifanayo: ukubiza uNksz Tottenham, umhlali we-54 Berners Street. abanye bayalela ukuba kuthuthwe, bethi "ucela ukuthi i-post-chaise kanye [namahhashi] amane kube sekhaya ... ukuze amdlulisele esigabeni sokuqala ebheke eBath". Amanothi kosomabhizinisi afundeka ngokuthi “wayefisa ukukhuluma naye ngezindaba ezibalulekile”;[8] inguqulo eyathunyelwa kothisha bomdanso yabacela “ukuba bambize kusasa, phakathi kwehora lesibili nelesithathu, njengoba efisa ukuba amadodakazi akhe athole iziyalezo”.[9] Kuthunyelwe izincwadi kusihlalo weBhange lase-England kanye ne-East India Company zibacela ukuthi bashayele ukuze bezwe imininingwane yokukhwabanisa okuthinta izinhlangano zabo. UMbusi waseGloucester wathunyelwa inothi emcela ukuba ahambele umbhede wokufa womgcini womndeni. 

NgoNovemba 1810 Hook nomngane babehamba behla ngoBerners Street lapho ekhomba, ebonakala nje ngokungahleliwe, endlini engunombolo 54 futhi wabheja ukuthi angenza indawo ikheli okukhulunywa ngalo kakhulu eLondon. Umngani wakhe wamkhwelisa ebhejini kanti ibheja yayisethelwe igwinya elilodwa.

athungatha isigebengu futhi anikezwa umklomelo, kodwa kwanhlanga zimuka nomoya.[21] UHook wacabanga ukuthi kuwukuhlakanipha ukushiya idolobha elikhulu futhi wahlala amasonto ambalwa emaphandleni.[6] Ngo-1812 uHook wasolwa ngokukhohlisa; wavuma ngakho encwadini yakhe ye-semi-autobiographical yango-1835 ethi Gilbert Gurney 

[T]nakhu akukho okumnandi - yini enye eyenza umphumela kuBerner's Street? Ngiyindoda - ngikwenzile ... kopisha ihlaya, futhi liyayeka ukuba linye; - noma yisiphi isiphukuphuku singalingisa isibonelo esake sabekwa - kodwa ngokuqondene nomcabango nokuklama, ngicabanga ukuthi lokho bekuphelele. 

Inkohliso yaphindwa nakwamanye amadolobha aseBrithani kanye naseParis.[23] Yaba umthombo wogqozi lwama-comic ballads namakhathuni futhi kwakhulunywa ngayo kuma-pantomime iminyaka eminingana.[24] Ngemva kokufa kukaHook ngo-1841 umngane wakhe uNancy Matthews—unkosikazi womlingisi uCharles Matthew—wathi kwakungeyena uHook owayebangela leli nkohliso kodwa “insizwa esencane, manje ... engomunye wabefundisi abaqine kakhulu embusweni”.[2] Uqhube wathi nakuba uHook engazange enze inkohliso ku-Berners Street, ngaphambili wayeke wenza umgilingwane ofanayo esikalini esincane ku-Bedford Street. 

Akukaze kube nencazelo yokuthi kungani kwenziwa umkhonyovu, noma ngokukhethwa kukaNkk Tottenham—owesifazane ocebile onokuma okuhle emphakathini—njengesisulu sezehlakalo.[24][7] U-Graeme Harper, ebhala ku-Oxford Dictionary of National Biography ngo-2008, uthi ukukhetha kwenziwa ngokungahleliwe.[1] Intatheli nombhali uRobert Chambers wabhala ngo-1879 ukuthi kwakukhona intukuthelo phakathi kukaNkk Tottenham noHook nabangane bakhe, kodwa akazange anikeze imininingwane.

UHook walinda ukuze abukele u-Coates egibela umcimbi enqoleni yakhe, "elalele konke okucolisekileyo, ngenkemba enesici sedayimane nomoya kaLouis le Grand". U-Coates wamenywa ukuba angene, kodwa ngesikhathi ehlola ithikithi "wabikelwa ngesizotha ukuthi kwenzeke iphutha", wahoxa. I-Westminster 

Westminster iyindawo enkulu yokuhlala yeDolobha laseWestminster eCentral London, eNgilandi. Isuka eMfuleni iThames iye ku-Oxford Street futhi inezimpawu zomhlaba eziningi ezidumile, okuhlanganisa iSigodlo saseWestminster, iBuckingham Palace, iWestminster Abbey, iWestminster Cathedral, iTrafalgar Square kanye nengxenye enkulu yesikhungo samasiko saseWest End okuhlanganisa nendawo yokuzijabulisa yaseWest End yaseshashalazini. 

Igama (IsiNgisi Esidala: Westmynstre) [1] lisuka encazelweni engakahleleki yesonto le-abbey kanye nenqaba yasebukhosini yaseSt Peter's (Westminster Abbey), entshonalanga yeDolobha laseLondon (kuze kube yiNguquko yamaNgisi kuphinde kube khona i-Eastminster abbey, ngakolunye uhlangothi lweDolobha laseLondon, eMpumalanga End of London). Imvelaphi ye-abbey isukela phakathi kwekhulu lesi-7 nele-10, kodwa yanyukela ekuveleleni kuzwelonke lapho yakhiwa kabusha u-Edward the Confessor ngekhulu le-11. Ngokuthuthukiswa kwesigodlo esidala eduze kwe-abbey, iWestminster ibiyikhaya likahulumeni waseNgilandi kusukela cishe ngo-1200, futhi kusukela ngo-1707 uHulumeni wase-United Kingdom. Ngo-1539, yaba idolobha. 

I-Westminster ivamise ukusetshenziswa njengegama-ntonye ukubhekisa ePhalamende lase-United Kingdom, elihlala esigodlweni saseWestminster. 

I-Geography 
Physical Geography 
IDolobha neNkululeko yase-Westminster kanye nezinye izikhungo zokuphatha zase-Westminster zomlando (ngaphandle kweDolobha lesimanjemanje lase-Westminster, i-London Borough eyakhiwa ngo-1965) yanwetshwa isuka eMfuleni iThames yaya emgwaqeni omdala waseRoma osuka eDolobheni uye entshonalanga yeNgilandi, manje osekuyi-Oxford Street. 




I-Westminster ngaphambi kokuya emadolobheni. Umgwaqo waseRoma (u-Oxford Street wesimanje) ukhonjiswa phezulu oya ngasentshonalanga. 
I-Thorney Island yayiphakathi kwezingalo zoMfula iTyburn wangaphambili ekuhlanganeni kwawo noMfula iThames, kuyilapho umngcele osentshonalanga ne-Chelsea wakhiwa uMfula iWestbourne owalahleka ngokufanayo.[2] Ulayini womfula usakha (nokubuyekezwa okuncane kakhulu) imingcele yesifunda sesimanje esineRoyal Borough yaseKensington kanye neChelsea. 


Amapherishi nezindawo zeDolobha kanye neNkululeko yase-Westminster. I-Westbourne engezansi yakha ingxenye yomngcele osentshonalanga, kanye no-Oxford Street enyakatho. 
Enyakatho, kude nomfula, iWestminster yayihlanganisa umhlaba nhlangothi zombili zeWestbourne,ikakhulukazi i-Knightsbridge (kuhlanganise nezingxenye ze-Hyde Park entshonalanga yechibi i-Serpentine (ekuqaleni eyakhiwe ngokucekela phansi umfula) kanye neningi le-Kensington Gardens).


I-Westminster ihlanganisa izifunda ezingaphansi kwe-Soho, i-St James, i-Mayfair, i-Covent Garden (eyabiwe ne-Camden engumakhelwane), i-Pimlico, i-Victoria, i-Belgravia ne-Knightsbridge (yabiwe ne-Kensington engumakhelwane). 

Idolobha langaphambili laseWestminster lahlangana nezindawo ezingomakhelwane zasePaddington kanye neMarylebone ngo-1965 ukwakha idolobha elikhulu lesimanje. Lezi zindawo ezingomakhelwane (ngaphandle kwendawo encane yasePaddington engxenyeni ye-Kensington Gardens), zisenyakatho yomgwaqo u-Oxford kanye nokuqhubeka kwawo ngasentshonalanga, u-Bayswater Road. 


















IBerners Street iyindawo esenyakatho yomgwaqo u-Oxford eDolobheni laseWestminster eWest End of London, eyasungulwa okokuqala njengomgwaqo wokuhlala abantu maphakathi nekhulu le-18 ngumakhi wezakhiwo uWilliam Berners, futhi kamuva yanikezelwa ezakhiweni ezinkulu zezentengiselwano nezama-semi-industrial noma ezindlini zamafulethi. Inobudlelwane noCharles Dickens, futhi bekuyindawo yabenzi bezinsimbi zomculo okuhlanganisa amapiyano namahabhu, kanye nefenisha nabakhi bamafilimu.

KwakukuBerners Street lapho, esengumfana, uDickens abona khona owesifazane owayezulazula, okwakusekelwe phezu kwakhe umlingiswa onguMiss Havisham ovela ku-Great Expectations. U-Dickens wamchaza "njengesidalwa esidala esizidlayo, esibandayo futhi esihlelekile" "esasigqoke ngokuphelele esimhlophe sinokwekwe okumhlophe okwesabekayo ekhanda laso nasebusweni ngaphakathi kwebhonethi yaso emhlophe." Wengeza ngokuthi "wahlanya ngenxa yezizathu zakhe eyedwa - ngokungangabazeki ngenxa yokuthi isicebi somQuaker sasingafuni ukumshada. Lena ingubo yakhe yomshado. Uhlale ehambela lapha… sibona esinyathelweni sakhe esicwebezelayo neso eliwufifi ukuthi uhlose ukumbulala." Lokhu kwakusendatshana yakhe ethi, “Lapho Sayeka Ukukhula” eyanyatheliswa ku-Household Words ngo-1 January 1853. 

Kamuva ekuphileni, e-31 Berners Street, u-Dickens wafaka isithandwa sakhe esincane esiyimfihlo, umlingisi u-Ellen Lawless Ternan (ogama lakhe linguNelly), owathi eneminyaka engu-19 wayemncane ngeminyaka engu-27 kuye, futhi eneminyaka efanayo nendodakazi yakhe endala. KwakunguSeptember 1858 lapho engena ephelezelwa unina uFrancis, owayengumlingisi, nodadewabo ababili, uMaria, omunye umlingisi, noFanny, umculi. Ngemva kwenyanga nje u-Ellen noMaria babika kuye ukuthi babehlushwa iphoyisa, uDickens ayesola ukuthi lalifumbathiswe indoda enesithakazelo sobulili kubo bobabili noma kubo bobabili. Ukhale ngalokhu “kuziphatha okungavamile, okuyingozi nokungafuneki kwephoyisa” acabanga ukuthi kufanele lichithwe. Kodwa ukwesaba ukwaziwa kwamvimbela ukuba aqhubeke nalo. IBhange LaseNgilandi yibhange elikhulu lase-United Kingdom kanye nemodeli okusekelwe kuyo amabhange amakhulu esimanje. Isungulwe ngo-1694 ukuze isebenze njengomabhange kanye nomphathi wezikweletu kuHulumeni wamaNgisi, futhi isengomunye wabamabhange kahulumeni wase-United Kingdom, iyibhange elikhulu lesibili elidala emhlabeni.

Ibhange laliphethwe ngasese ngabanikazi bamasheya kusukela ekusungulweni kwalo ngo-1694 laze lafakwa ngaphansi kombuso ngo-1946 yinkonzo yase-Attlee.[4] Ngo-1998 yaba inhlangano yomphakathi ezimele, ephethwe ngokuphelele nguSolicitor Wezezimali egameni likahulumeni, [5] enegunya lokusekela izinqubomgomo zezomnotho zikahulumeni wangaleso sikhathi, [6] kodwa ezimele ekugcineni ukuqina kwamanani.[7] Ngekhulu lama-21 ibhange lathatha umthwalo wemfanelo owengeziwe wokugcina nokuqapha uzinzo lwezezimali e-UK, futhi liya ngokuya lisebenza njengomlawuli osemthethweni. 

Ikomkhulu laleli bhange lisesifundeni esikhulu sezimali saseLondon, iDolobha laseLondon, kusukela ngo-1694, kanye naku-Threadneedle Street kusukela ngo-1734. Ngezinye izikhathi laziwa ngokuthi "I-Old Lady of Threadneedle Street", igama elithathwe kukhathuni ehlekisayo ka-James Gillray ngo-1797. Impambano yomgwaqo ngaphandle yaziwa ngokuthi i-Bank Junction. 

Ibhange, phakathi kwezinye izinto, lingumgadi wezinqolobane zegolide ezisemthethweni zase-United Kingdom (nalezo zakwamanye amazwe angaba ngu-30).[10] Kusukela ngo-Ephreli 2016, ibhange labamba amathani angaba ngu-5,134 (amathani angama-5,659) egolide, abiza u-£141 billion. Lezi zilinganiso ziphakamisa ukuthi i-vault ingabamba cishe u-3% wamathani ayi-171,300 egolide ambiwa kuwo wonke umlando wesintu. 

Imisebenzi 
Ngokuvumelana ne-strapline yalo, inhloso eyinhloko yebhange 'ukuthuthukisa okuhle kwabantu base-United Kingdom ngokugcina uzinzo lwezimali nezezimali'. Lokhu kufezwa ngezindlela ezihlukahlukene: 

Ukuzinza kwezimali 
Izintengo ezizinzile nezindlela zokukhokha ezivikelekile yizindlela ezimbili eziyinhloko zokuzinza kwezimali. 

Amanani aqinile 
Izintengo ezizinzile zigcinwa ngokufuna ukuqinisekisa ukuthi ukwenyuka kwamanani kufinyelela imigomo kaHulumeni yokwehla kwamandla emali. Ibhange lihlose ukufeza lokhu okuhlosiwe ngokulungisa izinga lenzalo eliyisisekelo (elaziwa ngokuthi izinga lasebhange), elinqunywa iKomidi Lenqubomgomo Yezimali yebhange (MPC). (I-MPC inikeze umthwalo wemfanelo wokuphatha inqubomgomo yezimali; UMnyango Wezezimali we-HM unamandla agodliwe okunikeza imiyalo ekomitini "uma idingeka ngokwentshisekelo yomphakathi kanye nezimo zezomnotho ezimbi kakhulu", kodwa iPhalamende kufanele ligunyaze leyo miyalo zingakapheli izinsuku ezingama-28.) 

Kusukela ngo-2024 umgomo wokwehla kwamandla emali u-2%; uma lokhu okuhlosiwe kweqiwe uMbusi kudingeka abhale incwadi evulelekile eya kuShansela Womnyango Wezimali echaza isimo futhi aphakamise amakhambi.[15] Ngaphandle kokusetha izinga lenzalo eliyisisekelo, ithuluzi eliyinhloko elisetshenziswa yibhange kulokhu ukuncishiswa komthamo.
 
Vikela izindlela zokukhokha
Ibhange linegunya phezu kodaba lwamanothi eNgilandi naseWales futhi lilawula ukukhishwa kwamabhange ngamabhange ezentengiselwano eScotland naseNyakatho Ireland. (Amabhange ase-Scottish nase-Northern Irish agcina ilungelo lokukhipha ama-banknotes awo, kodwa kufanele axhaswe umuntu ngamunye ngediphozithi ebhange, ngaphandle kwamaphawundi ayizigidi ezimbalwa amelela inani lamanothi ayenawo ngo-1845.) 

Ngaphezu kwalokho ibhange liqondisa ezinye izinhlelo zokukhokha, ezisebenza njenge-ejenti yokukhokhelana kanye nezinhlelo ze-Real-time settlement ze-CHAPS.[18] Ngo-2024 ibhange lalikhokha imali elinganiselwa ku-£500 billion phakathi kwamabhange usuku nosuku. 

Ukuzinza kwezezimali 
Ukugcina uzinzo kwezezimali kuhilela ukuvikela abongi base-UK, abatshalizimali kanye nababolekayo ekusongelweni kohlelo lwezezimali lulonke.[8] Izinsongo zitholwa umsebenzi webhange wokugada nezobunhloli bemakethe, futhi zisingathwa ngokusebenza kwezezimali nokunye (kokubili ekhaya naphesheya). Iningi lalezi zindlela zokuvikela zafakwa ngemuva kwenkinga yezimali yango-2008: 

Umthetho 
Ngo-2011 i-Prudential Regulation Authority yebhange yasungulwa ukuze ilawule futhi yengamele wonke amabhange amakhulu, izinhlangano zokwakha, izinyunyana zezikweletu, izinkampani zomshuwalense kanye namafemu okutshala izimali e-UK ('i-microprudential regulation').[19] Ibhange liphinde libe neqhaza elisemthethweni lokuqondisa mayelana nengqalasizinda yezimakethe zezimali. 

Ukulawulwa kobungozi 
Ngesikhathi esifanayo, iKomidi Lenqubomgomo Yezezimali (i-FPC) yebhange lamiswa ukuze lihlonze futhi liqaphe ubungozi ohlelweni lwezezimali, futhi lithathe izinyathelo ezifanele lapho kunesidingo ('imithethonqubo ebanzi'). I-FPC ishicilela lokho ekutholile (kanye nezinyathelo ezithathiwe) Kumbiko Wokusimama Kwezezimali waminyaka yonke kabili ngonyaka. 

Amasevisi ebhange 
Ibhange lihlinzeka ngezinsizakalo zamabhange amakhulu kuHulumeni wase-UK (kanye namabhange amakhulu angaphezu kwekhulu aphesheya kwezilwandle).[22] Iphatha I-Akhawunti Ye-Exchange Equalization yase-UK egameni lika-HM Treasury futhi inakekela i-akhawunti kahulumeni Yesikhwama Esihlanganisiwe.[23] Iphinde ilawule izindawo zokushintshiselana ngemali yangaphandle futhi ingumlondolozi wezinqolobane zegolide zase-UK (kanye nabanye). 

Ibhange liphinde linikeze 'ukwesekwa kwezimali kanye nezinye izinsiza emabhange nakwezinye izikhungo zezezimali'.[13] Amabhange ezentengiselwano ngokuvamile agcina ingxenye enkulu yezimali zawo ezigciniwe lapho efakwa eBhange LaseNgilandi. Lezi zimali ezigciniwe zamabhange amakhulu zisetshenziswa amabhange ukukhokhelana izinkokhelo zodwa;[25] (ngalesi sizathu iBhange LaseNgilandi ngezinye izikhathi libizwa ngokuthi 'ibhange lababhange').[26] Ezimweni ezingavamile, iBhange lingase libe ngumbolekisi wendlela yokugcina ngokwelula isikweletu uma kungekho esinye isikhungo esingakwenza.

Njengomlawuli kanye nebhange elikhulu, iBhange LaseNgilandi alikaze linikeze izinsiza zebhange zabathengi iminyaka eminingi, kodwa lisalawula ezinye izinkonzo ezibhekene nomphakathi (njengokushintshana ngemali yamanothi ebhange athathelwe indawo).[27] Kuze kube ngu-2017, izisebenzi zeBhange zazinelungelo lokuvula ama-akhawunti amanje ngokuqondile neBhange LaseNgilandi futhi zanikezwa ikhodi yohlobo eyingqayizivele ethi 10-00-00. 

Isinqumo 
Ngaphansi kwemigomo yoMthetho Wamabhange ka-2009 ibhange liyiSigungu Seziphathimandla sase-UK sanoma yiliphi ibhange noma umphakathi wezakhiwo ohlulelwa 'mkhulu kakhulu ukuba ungahluleka'; ngalokho inikwa amandla okwenza uma kwenzeka ibhange lihluleka 'ukuvikela izinsiza ezibalulekile zezezimali zase-UK nokuzinza kwezezimali'. 

Izinsizakalo zomlando nezibopho 
Phakathi kuka-1715 no-1998, iBhange LaseNgilandi laliphethe Izitokwe Zikahulumeni (ezakhe ingxenye enkulu yesikweletu sikazwelonke): ibhange lalinomthwalo wemfanelo wokukhipha amasheya kubanikazi bamasheya, ukukhokha izinzuzo kanye nokugcina irejista yokudluliswa kwemali;[26] nokho ngo-1998, ngemva kwesinqumo sokunikeza isikweletu, ukuphathwa kwebhange kwakunomthwalo wemfanelo wokudlulisa ukuzimela kweHhovisi elisha. iphinde yathatha i-Exchequer yokuphatha imali kanye nesibopho sokukhipha izikweletu zikaMgcinimafa ebhange ngo-2000.[30] I-Computershare ithathe isikhundla sokubhalisa amabhondi kahulumeni wase-UK (izibambiso eziqinile noma 'ama-gilts') ebhange ekupheleni kuka-2004. Ibhange, nokho, liyaqhubeka nokusebenza njenge-ejenti yokukhokhela izikweletu zeHhovisi Lokuphathwa Kwezikweletu kanye nomgcini wezibambiso zalo. 

Kusukela lasungulwa ngo-1694, leli bhange lalihlinzekele uHulumeni insizakalo yamabhange okudayisa; nokho ngo-2008 yanquma ukuhoxa ekunikezeni lezi zinsizakalo, manje ezihlinzekwa uhla lwezinye izikhungo zezimali futhi ezilawulwa yi-Government Banking Service. 

Kuze kube ngu-2016, ibhange belihlinzeka ngezinsizakalo zebhange lomuntu siqu njengelungelo lezisebenzi.[28] Ngaphambilini, ibhange beligcina ama-akhawunti angasese nawezohwebo azo zonke izinhlobo zamakhasimende, okuhlanganisa abantu ngabanye, amabhizinisi amancane kanye nezinhlangano zomphakathi; kodwa ukushintsha kwenqubomgomo okwalandela iMpi Yezwe Yokuqala kwabona ukuthi ibhange liya ngokuya lihoxa kulolu hlobo lwebhizinisi ukuze ligxile ngokucace kakhulu endimeni yalo yamabhange.

Amabhomu amabili e-suffragette aboniswe e-City of London Police Museum ngo-2019. Ibhomu elingakwesobunxele lasetshenziswa ekuzameni ukuqhuma kwamabhomu ngaphandle kwebhange ngomhla ka-4 ku-April 1913, ukuhlasela okungenzeka kwadala ukulimala okuningi ukube akuzange kuvinjwe.

Ngesikhathi sokuphatha kukaMontagu Norman, kusukela ngo-1920 kuya ku-1944, ibhange lenza imizamo eqondile yokusuka emabhange okuhweba libe yibhange elikhulu. Umbusi wakamuva, uRobin Leigh-Pemberton, wasichaza 'njengesikhathi soshintsho olusheshayo, lapho saqala khona ukuqhela emasikweni abefundisi beminyaka engu-200 [...] futhi samukele uchwepheshe, umshini kanye neziyalo zokuphatha zesimanje'.[46] Osomnotho kanye nezibalo baqale ukuqashwa ebhange ngenani elikhulayo. Ngo-1931 'Ikomidi Lepigogo', elasungulwa ukuze leluleke ngokuthuthukiswa kwenhlangano, lashicilela izincomo ezihlanganisa ukuqokwa kwabaqondisi abakhulu abakhokhelwayo (kanye namalungu endabuko angewona aphethe eNkantolo). Iphinde yancoma ukumiswa kabusha kwezinhlaka zomnyango ezijwayelekile zebhange. 


Ibhange lika-Baker elakhiwe kabusha limi ngemuva kodonga lwamakhethini luka-Soane olugcinelwe ingxenye 
Umsebenzi webhange usukhule kakhulu kusukela ekupheleni kweMpi Yezwe Yokuqala, futhi isinqumo sathathwa sokunwetshwa. Phakathi kuka-1925 no-1939 indlunkulu yebhange ku-Threadneedle Street yakhiwa kabusha ngokuphelele ngu-Herbert Baker. (Lokhu kwakuhilela ukubhidlizwa kwezakhiwo eziningi zikaSir John Soane, isenzo esichazwe isazi-mlando sezokwakha u-Nikolaus Pevsner “njengobugebengu obukhulu kakhulu bezakhiwo, eDolobheni laseLondon, ekhulwini lamashumi amabili.)[82] Ekuqaleni icebo bekuwukugcina amahholo amabhange ka-Soane ngemuva kodonga lwamakhethini, kodwa lokhu kwafakazela inselele futhi kwaba inselele. Ukubhidliza nokwakhiwa kabusha kwenzeke ngezigaba, abasebenzi besuka kwenye ingxenye yesakhiwo beya kwenye (noma, kwezinye izimo, bayohlala endaweni yesikhashana e-Finsbury Circus). Inkunzi nezibambiso zahlala endaweni yonke. Ngesikhathi kwakhiwa kabusha izinsalela zabantu eziphathelene negceke lesonto elidala lase-St Christopher le Stocks zambiwa futhi zangcwatshwa kabusha emathuneni aseNunhead.

Ibhange laphishekela imigomo eminingi yezomnotho zaseKeynesian ngemuva kuka-1945, ikakhulukazi "imali elula" kanye namazinga enzalo ephansi ukusekela ukufunwa okuhlanganisiwe. Izamile ukugcina izinga lokushintshanisa okungaguquki futhi yazama ukubhekana nokwehla kwamandla emali kanye nobuthakathaka be-sterling ngezilawuli zekhredithi kanye nokuhwebelana.

Umnyango esikhundleni salokho wanikezwa indawo yokuhlala yesikhashana (ephinde e-Finsbury Circus), kusalindwe ukwakhiwa kwebhilidi elisha, elizothatha indawo yebhomu engamahektare amabili empumalanga ye-St Paul's Cathedral. 'I-Bank of England New Change' yaklanywa u-Victor Heal futhi yavulwa ngo-1957 (ngaleso sikhathi kwakuyiphrojekthi enkulu yaseLondon yokwakha kabusha ngemva kwempi);[86] isakhiwo esisha sasiqukethe izinsiza zabasebenzi ezimbalwa eceleni kwehhovisi lokuhlala futhi, ezingeni lomgwaqo, amayunithi okudayisa avunyelwe ukuhlukahluka kwamabhizinisi. Ibhange lalinesakhiwo esinesivumelwano sokuqashisa seminyaka engu-200; kodwa ngokufika kwezinombolo zabasebenzi bekhompyutha zehliswa kakhulu ngeminyaka yawo-1980-90s; izingxenye zesakhiwo zavunyelwa kwezinye izimboni (ikakhulukazi inkampani yabameli i-Allen & Overy). Ibhange ladayisa lesi sakhiwo ngo-2000 kwathi ngo-2007 sabhidlizwa; Ushintsho Olusha Olulodwa manje lumi kusayithi.

Ngo-1977 ibhange lasungula inkampani ephethwe ngokugcwele ebizwa ngokuthi i-Bank of England Nominees Limited (BOEN), okuyinkampani ezimele engasekho, enamasheya ayo amabili e-£1 akhishiwe. Ngokwememorandamu yayo yenhlangano, izinhloso zayo bekuwukuthi: "Ukusebenza njengoPhathiwe noma i-ejenti noma ummeli ngokuhlanganyela noma ngokuhlanganyela nabanye, kunoma yimuphi umuntu noma abantu, ubambiswano, inkampani, inhlangano, uhulumeni, umbuso, inhlangano, umbuso, isifundazwe, igunya, noma inhlangano yomphakathi, nanoma yiliphi iqembu noma inhlangano yabo". I-Bank of England Nominees Limited inikezwe ukukhululwa ngu-Edmund Dell, Unobhala Wezohwebo Wezwe, ezimfuneko zokudalulwa ngaphansi kweSigaba 27(9) soMthetho Wezinkampani 1976, ngenxa yokuthi "kwakubhekwa njengokungathandeki ukuthi izimfuneko zokudalulwa kufanele zisebenze ezigabeni ezithile zabanikazi bamasheya". IBhange LaseNgilandi futhi livikelwe yisikhundla salo sombuso kanye noMthetho Wezimfihlo Ezisemthethweni.[87] I-BOEN bekuyimoto yohulumeni nezinhloko zezifundazwe ukuthi batshale izimali ezinkampanini zase-UK (kuncike ekugunyazweni kukaNobhala Wezwe), inqobo nje uma bezibophezela "ukungabi nomthelela ezindabeni zenkampani".[88] Eminyakeni yayo yakamuva, i-BOEN yayingasakhululekile ezimfuneko zokudalulwa komthetho wenkampani.[89] Nakuba kuyinkampani ethule,[90] ukuhlala kahle akuvimbeli inkampani esebenza ngokugcwele njengomnikazi wamasheya oqokiwe.[91] I-BOEN yayinabanikazi bamasheya ababili: iBhange LaseNgilandi, kanye noNobhala WeBhange LaseNgilandi. 

Imfuneko egciniwe yokuthi amabhange abambe ingxenye encane emisiwe yezimali ezifakwayo njengezigodlo eBhange LaseNgilandi yaqedwa ngo-1981: bheka imfuneko ye-Reserve § United Kingdom ukuze uthole imininingwane eyengeziwe. Ushintsho lwamanje olusuka kwezomnotho lwaseKeynesian luye kwezomnotho lwaseChicago lwahlaziywa nguNicholas Kaldor encwadini ethi The Scourge of Monetarism. 

Ukunikezelwa kwenqubomgomo yezimali ebhange kwaba ipulani eliyinhloko lenqubomgomo yezomnotho ye-Liberal Democrats okhethweni lukazwelonke lwango-1992.[94] Ilungu le-Conservative MP u-Nicholas Budgen naye wayephakamise lokhu njengomthethosivivinywa welungu elizimele ngo-1996, kodwa lo mthethosivivinywa wehluleka njengoba wawungasekelwanga uhulumeni noma abaphikisayo. 

Uhulumeni wase-UK washiya i-European Exchange Rate Mechanism ebizayo ukuze igcinwe ngo-September 1992, isenzo esabiza i-HM Treasury ngaphezu kwe-£3 billion. Lokhu kuholele ekutheni kube nokuxhumana phakathi kukahulumeni naleli bhange.

Ngo-1993, ibhange lakhiqizela uhulumeni umbiko walo wokuqala we-Inflation, ochaza kabanzi ngezimo zokwehla kwamandla emali kanye nezingcindezi. Lo mbiko okhiqizwa minyaka yonke uhlala ungomunye wezincwadi ezinkulu zebhange.[84] Impumelelo yokuqondisa ukwehla kwamandla emali e-United Kingdom kudalwe ukugxila kwebhange ekwenziweni kwezinto obala.[95] IBhange LaseNgilandi libe ngumholi ekukhiqizeni izindlela ezintsha zokudlulisela ulwazi emphakathini, ikakhulukazi ngombiko walo Wokukhuphuka Kwamanani Emali,okuyinto amanye amabhange amakhulu amaningi alingise.

Ibhange lagubha usuku lwalo lokuzalwa lamakhulu amathathu ngo-1994. 

Ngo-1996, ibhange lakhiqiza i-Financial Stability Review yokuqala. Le ncwadi yonyaka yaziwa ngokuthi i-Financial Stability Report ngo-2006. [84] Futhi ngalowo nyaka, ibhange lasungula uhlelo lwalo lwe-real-time gross settlement (RTGS) ukuze lithuthukise ukukhokhelana okungenabungozi phakathi kwamabhange ase-UK. 

Mhla ziyisi-6 kuNhlaba 1997, kulandela ukhetho lukazwelonke lwango-1997 olwabeka uhulumeni Wezabasebenzi okokuqala ngqa kusukela ngo-1979, kwamenyezelwa nguShansela we-Exchequer, uGordon Brown, ukuthi ibhange lizonikezwa inkululeko yokusebenza phezu kwenqubomgomo yezimali.[97] Ngaphansi kwemigomo yoMthetho weBhange LaseNgilandi ka-1998 (owaqala ukusebenza mhla lu-1 kuNhlangulana 1998) iKomidi Lenqubomgomo Yezimali (MPC) lanikezwa umthwalo wemfanelo wodwa wokusetha izilinganiso zenzalo ukuze kuhlangatshezwane nemigomo yokwehla kwamandla emali kaHulumeni ye-Retail Prices Index (RPI) engu-2.5%. [98] Okuhlosiwe kushintshile kwaba ngu-2% kusukela Inkomba Yentengo Yabathengi (CPI) ithathele indawo Inkomba Yezintengo Zokudayisa njengenkomba yokwehla kwamandla emali yoMgcinimafa.[99] Uma ukwehla kwamandla emali kweqa noma kwehlisa okuhlosiwe ngaphezu kuka-1% uMbusi kufanele abhalele uShansela we-Exchequer incwadi echaza ukuthi kungani, nokuthi uzosilungisa kanjani lesi simo. 

Amabhange amakhulu azimele asebenzisa i-inflation target aziwa ngokuthi ama-Friedmanite central banks. Lolu shintsho kupolitiki Yezabasebenzi lwachazwa ngu-Skidelsky kwethi Ukubuya Kwengcweti[101] njengephutha futhi njengokwamukelwa kwenkolelo-mbono yokulindela okunengqondo njengoba yamenyezelwa u-Alan Walters.[102] Izinhloso zokwehla kwamandla emali kuhlanganiswe nokuzimela kwamabhange amakhulu kuphawulwe njengesu "lokubulala isilo" elidala ukuntuleka kwemali embonini kahulumeni. 

ngoJuni 1998 umsebenzi wokulawula nokuqondisa amabhange kanye nezimboni zomshwalense wasuswa ebhange wayiswa ku-Financial Services Authority. Imemorandamu yokuqonda ichaze imigomo ibhange, uMnyango Wezezimali, kanye ne-FSA ezosebenzela ngaphansi kwayo ukuze kufezeke inhloso efanayo yokwandisa uzinzo kwezezimali.[103] (Eminyakeni eyishumi kamuva, nokho, ngemva kwenhlekelele yezimali yango-2008, umthetho omusha wamabhange wadlulisela umthwalo wemfanelo wokulawula nokuqondisa izimboni zamabhange nezomshwalense emuva eBhange LaseNgilandi).

Ngo-2010, iShansela engenayo yamemezela inhloso yayo yokuhlanganisa i-Financial Services Authority ibuyele ebhange Ngo-2011 kwasungulwa iKomidi leNqubomgomo Yezimali yesikhashana (i-FPC) (njengekomidi lesibuko leKomidi Lenqubomgomo Yezimali) ukuze lihole igunya elisha lebhange mayelana nokusimama kwezezimali, i-FPC kanye nemisebenzi ye-Financial Services I-Prudential Regulation Authority (PRA), kanye namandla amaningi okuqondisa abahlinzeki bengqalasizinda yemakethe

. yasebenza, kusukela ngoJanuwari 2009, i-Asset Purchase Facility (APF) yokuthenga "impahla yekhwalithi ephezulu exhaswe yi-Treasury bills kanye nemisebenzi yokuphatha ukheshi ye-DMO" futhi ngaleyo ndlela ithuthukise i-liquidity ezimakethe zezikweletu.[112] I-MPC Kanye nokuphatha izimali ze-QE, ezazingu-£895 bn phezulu, i-APF iyaqhubeka nokusebenzisa izakhiwo zayo zezinkampani Zombili zenziwa inkampani engaphansi kweBhange LaseNgilandi, i-Bank of England Asset Purchase Facility Fund Limited (BEAPFF).

I-QE yaklanywa ngokuyinhloko njengethuluzi lenqubomgomo yezimali. Indlela yokusebenza yayidinga ukuthi iBhange LaseNgilandi lithenge amabhondi kahulumeni emakethe yesibili, ekhokhelwa ngokudala imali entsha yasebhange eliphakathi. Lokhu kuzoba nomthelela wokwenyusa izintengo zempahla yamabhondi athengiwe, ngaleyo ndlela kwehlise isivuno futhi kwehliswe izinga lenzalo lesikhathi eside. Inhloso yale nqubomgomo ekuqaleni bekuwukudambisa imikhawulo yemali etholakalayo ohlelweni lwe-sterling reserves kodwa yashintsha yaba umgomo obanzi wokuhlinzeka ngesikhuthazo somnotho. 

I-QE yenziwe ngezigaba eziyisithupha phakathi kuka-2009 no-2020. Ekuphakameni kwayo ngo-2020, iphothifoliyo yafinyelela ku-£895 billion, ehlanganisa u-£875 billion wamabhondi kahulumeni wase-UK kanye no-£20 webhiliyoni wamabhondi okuhweba ezinga eliphezulu. 

NgoFebhuwari 2022, iBhange LaseNgilandi lamemezela inhloso yalo yokuqala ukuvala iphothifoliyo ye-QE.[113] Ekuqaleni lokhu bekuzofezwa ngokungashintshi izilinganiso zamabhondi avuthwayo, futhi kamuva kwakuzosheshiswa ngokuthengiswa kwebhondi okusebenzayo. 

Ngo-Agasti 2022, iBhange Lase-England laphinda laphinda inhloso yalo yokusheshisa ukwehla kwe-QE ngokuthengisa okusebenzayo kwebhondi. Le nqubomgomo yaqinisekiswa ngokushintshana kwezincwadi phakathi kweBhange LaseNgilandi kanye Neshansela lase-UK le-Exchequer ngoSepthemba 2022.[114] Phakathi kukaFebhuwari 2022 noSepthemba 2022, isamba esingu-£37.1bn wamabhondi kahulumeni avuthwa, ehlisa isitoko esisele sisuka ku-£875.0bn ekupheleni kuka-2021 saya ku-£837.9bn. Ngaphezu kwalokho, isamba esingu-£1.1bn samabhondi ezinkampani sivuthwe, sehlisa isitoko sisuka ku-£20.0bn saya ku-£18.9bn, kanti ukuthengiswa kwesitoko esisele kuhlelwe ukuthi kuqale ngomhla zingama-27 kuSepthemba.

Isihloko esiyinhloko: Iphepha lebhange lase-England likhipha 

inothi elingu-£1 langonyaka ka-1797 (futhi elamakwa ngokuthi likhokhwe ngo-1928) 
Ibhange likhiphe amanothi kusukela ngo-1694. Amanothi ayebhalwa ngesandla ekuqaleni; nakuba zanyatheliswa ngokwengxenye kusukela ngo-1725 kuqhubeke, osokheshi kwakusadingeka basayine inothi ngalinye futhi babenze bakhokhele othile. Amanothi anyatheliswa ngokugcwele kusukela ngo-1855. Kuze kube ngu-1928 wonke amanothi "Amanothi Amhlophe", aphrintiwe ngombala omnyama futhi angenalutho. Ngekhulu le-18 nele-19, Amanothi Amhlophe akhishwa ngamahlelo ka-£1 no-£2. Phakathi nekhulu lama-20, Amanothi Amhlophe akhishwa ngamahlelo aphakathi kuka-£5 no-£1000. Kuze kube kuphasiswa uMthetho Ojwayelekile Wegolide ka-1925 ibhange lalibophekile ukuthi likhokhe uma lifunwa inani lemali yemali yegolide kumphathi walo. 

Ngo-1724 ibhange langena esivumelwaneni no-Henry Portal waseWhitchurch, eHamphire ukuhlinzeka ngephepha lezinga eliphezulu lokunyathelisa imali engamaphepha.[115] Ukunyathelisa ngokwako kwenziwa yizinkampani ezizimele zokunyathelisa; izingcwecwe zethusi zazigcinwa egumbini elingaphansi, futhi phakathi nesikhathi sazo emshinini wokunyathelisa ngumabhalane wasebhange (owayezobhala inani lamakhophi enziwe); uma komile babezohanjiswa ebhange. Umsebenzi wokunyathelisa walethwa emagcekeni ebhange (nakuba wawusengaphansi kwenkontileka yangasese) ngo-1791; ngo-1808 yalethwa ngokugcwele endlini.


Iphepha lemali lase-One Guinea, elakhishwa iGloucester Old Bank ngo-1813 
Kuze kube maphakathi nekhulu le-19, amabhange ezentengiselwano ayevunyelwe ukuba akhiphe awawo amabhange, futhi amanothi akhishwa izinkampani zamabhange zesifundazwe ayevame ukusakazwa.[116] I-Bank Charter Act 1844 yaqala inqubo yokukhawulela udaba lwamanothi ebhange; amabhange amasha ayenqatshelwe ukukhipha awawo amaphepha emali, futhi amabhange akhona akhipha amaphepha ayengavunyelwe ukwandisa udaba lwawo. Njengoba izinkampani zamabhange zezifundazwe zazihlangana ukuze zenze amabhange amakhulu, zalahlekelwa ilungelo lazo lokukhipha amanothi, futhi imali yangasese yaseNgilandi yagcina inyamalele, kwashiya ibhange nomthwalo wemfanelo wezindaba zamanothi eNgilandi naseWales. Ibhange lokugcina elizimele ukukhipha awalo amanothi e-England naseWales kwakunguFox, Fowler and Company ngo-1921.[117] Kodwa-ke, ukulinganiselwa koMthetho we-1844 kuthinte kuphela amabhange aseNgilandi naseWales, futhi namuhla amabhange amathathu okuhweba eScotland namathathu aseNyakatho Ireland ayaqhubeka nokukhipha ama-banknotes awo, alawulwa yibhange.

La manothi abe nesithombe seNkosi uGeorge V (Amanothi eBhange laseNgilandi awazange aqale ukubonisa isithombe senkosi kuze kube ngu-1960). Amagama anothi ngalinye ayethi:

Iphepha elithi 'Operation Bernhard' £50, elaqanjwa iziboshwa ekamu lokuhlushwa laseSachsenhausen 
Phakathi neMpi Yezwe Yesibili, umkhankaso waseJalimane u-Operation Bernhard wazama ukwenza amahlelo mbumbulu phakathi kuka-£5 no-£50, okwenza imali engu-500,000 ibe yimali ye-parachu yenyanga ngayinye e-UK. umzamo wokuthunaza umnotho waseBrithani, kodwa kwatholakala kuwusizo kakhulu ukusebenzisa amanothi ukukhokhela ama-agent aseJalimane asebenza kulo lonke elaseYurophu. Nakuba iningi lawela ezandleni zama-Allied ekupheleni kwempi, amaphutha ayevame ukuvela iminyaka eminingi kamuva, okwaholela ekutheni amahlelo emali engaphezu kuka-£5 asuswe ekusakazweni.

Ngo-1917 ibhange lase lithuthe umsebenzi walo wokunyathelisa layiswa eSt Luke's Printing Works, okwakuyisibhedlela sangaphambili; ngo-1958 yathuthela eDebden. Amanothi esimanje aphrintwa ngenkontileka neDe La Rue Currency eLoughton, Essex. 

Amahhovisi egatsha 

iBhange LaseNgilandi, e-King Street, eLeeds: esinye sezakhiwo eziningi zegatsha ezinonya esavulwa ekuqaleni kwawo-1970. 
Esikhathini esiningi sekhulunyaka leshumi nesishiyagalolunye namashumi amabili ibhange lalinamagatsha amaningi eLondon nakwamanye amadolobha aseNgilandi. 

Amagatsha okuqala avulwa ngo-1826 (ngomfutho onikezwe ukuphasiswa kwe-Country Bankers Act, okwavumela okokuqala ukusungulwa kwamabhange esitoko ngaphandle kwaseLondon).[75] Ibhange lalibheke ukuthi uxhaxha olusha lwamagatsha 'luzokwandisa ukusetshenziswa kwama-Bank Notes, lunikeze ibhange ukulawula okuphelele kakhulu kuwo wonke amaphepha asakazwayo, futhi livikele ibhange emiqhudelwaneni yezinkampani zamabhange ezinkulu'.[121] Igatsha ngalinye laliqondiswa i-Agent (umuntu 'onolwazi lwezentengiselwano, onolwazi lwasendaweni').[75] Ngo-1829 kwakunamagatsha ayishumi nanye asebenza (ayishumi eNgilandi nelilodwa eWales). Amanye amagatsha angenanzuzo encane awahlali isikhathi esifushane, kodwa amanye aqhubeka nokusebenza kwaze kwaba ngawo-1990. 

Ngo-1997 amagatsha amahlanu okugcina asele avalwa; ama-Agent, nokho, agcinwa, ngohlaka lwama-Regional Agencies adalwe (kulo lonke elase-UK), amanye awo ayezinze ezakhiweni zamagatsha zangaphambili.
