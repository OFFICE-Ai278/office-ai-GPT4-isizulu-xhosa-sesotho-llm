Ubuqhophololo beSitrato saseBerners benziwa ngumbhali uTheodore Hook eWestminster (ngoku eyinxalenye yeLondon) ngowe-1810. Emva kweeveki eziliqela zokulungiselela wenza ubhejo oluzenzekelayo nomhlobo wakhe ukuba unokuguqula nayiphi na indawo ibe yeyona dilesi kwathethwa ngayo eLondon. UHook uchithe iiveki ezintandathu ethumela phakathi kwewaka elinesine iileta eziya kubarhwebi kunye namashishini eodola ukuhanjiswa kwempahla kunye neenkonzo zabo kwi-54 Berners Street, eWestminster, ngamaxesha ahlukeneyo ngomhla wama-27 kuNovemba 1810. Abantu abaliqela abaziwayo nabo bamenywa ukuba bafowunele kule dilesi, kuquka noosihlalo beBhanki yaseNgilani kunye neNkampani yase-East India, iDuke yaseLondon yaseGlouce Mayorce. 

UHook nabahlobo bakhe baqeshe amagumbi kwindlu ejongene nenombolo 54 ukuze bajonge inkqubo. Ukutshayela itshimini kwaqalisa ukufika kwidilesi ngeye-5:00 kusasa ngolo suku, kulandelwa ngamakhulu abameli bezorhwebo namashishini aliqela, kuquka abo bathengisa iifandesi, abangcwabi, abathengisa ukutya, abaxheshi, ababhaki, abapheki neengcaphephe zokudanisa; Ukuhanjiswa kwempahla kuquka amalungu omzimba, ifanitshala, amalahle, amaqebengwana omtshato, ukutya, iziselo kunye nebhokisi. Amapolisa abiziwe ukuba azame ukulawula isihlwele kodwa akakwazanga ukususa isitalato de kwaba semva kokuthontelana kokugqibela kweendwendwe ngo-5:00 pm: abasebenzi basekhaya abacinga ukuba baza kudliwano-ndlebe ukuze bafumane umsebenzi. 

UHook wayengachazwanga ngelo xesha, kodwa wavuma ukubandakanyeka kwakhe kwinoveli ye-semi-autobiographical epapashwe kwiminyaka engamashumi amabini anesihlanu emva kwesiganeko. Inkohliso yaphindwa kwiBritani yonke naseParis, kwaye yaphinda yaxelwa eqongeni, ngengoma kunye nangabazobi bekhathuni.

uTheodore Hook wayengumbhali wee-comic operas kunye neefarces zeqonga kunye nombhali weenoveli ezininzi. Wayenecala elikhohlakeleyo kwindalo yakhe kwaye wayesaziwa njenge-prankster kunye ne-hoaxer.[1] Umbhali-mbali uJudith Flanders uphawula ukuba wayesaziwa kakhulu ngeziqhulo zakhe ezisebenzayo njengoko wayesaziwa ngokubhala kwakhe.[2] Ngexesha le-Berners Street hoax wayeneminyaka engama-22 ubudala. 

Ucwangciso lukaHook ngobuqhetseba lwaqala ngo-Oktobha 1810, kwiiveki ezintandathu ngaphambi kokuba kwenzeke eso siganeko, yaye wayenabahlobo ababini ababeza kuncedisa kulo.[4] Akwaziwa ukuba ngoobani abo wayehamba nabo—indoda nebhinqa—kodwa kucingelwa ukuba omnye wabo kamva waba ngumdlali weqonga odumileyo.[5] Inani elikhulu leeleta zathunyelwa kubarhwebi, abathengisi kunye nabaziwayo kusetyenziswa izizathu ezahlukeneyo; Uqikelelo lwenani loonobumba luyahluka ukusuka "kwiwaka ubuncinane"[6] ukuya kumawaka amane.

Iingqinisiso ezisetyenzisiweyo zitshintshile kuxhomekeke ekubeni ngubani owayeqhagamshelwana naye, kodwa zonke zinemiyalelo efanayo: ukubiza uNksk Tottenham, umhlali we-54 Berners Street. abanye bayalele isithuthi, besithi "ucela ukuba i-post-chaise kunye [namahashe] amane abe sekhaya ... ukuze amdlulisele kwinqanaba lokuqala eliya eBhafini". Amanqaku awayesiya koosomashishini afundeka ukuba “wayenqwenela ukuthetha naye ngomcimbi obalulekileyo”;[8] inguqulelo eyathunyelwa kubafundisi-ntsapho bomdaniso yabacela “ukuba bambize ngomso, phakathi kweyure yesibini neyesithathu, njengoko enqwenela ukuba iintombi zakhe zifumane imiyalelo”.[9] Kuthunyelwe iileta koosihlalo beBhanki yaseNgilane kunye ne-East India Company becela ukuba batsalele umnxeba ukuva iinkcukacha zobuqhophololo obuchaphazela imibutho yabo. UMlawuli waseGloucester wathunyelwa incwadi emcela ukuba aye kukhuko lokufa lomgcini wosapho. 

NgoNovemba 1810 Hook kunye nomhlobo babehamba phantsi Berners Street xa walatha, ebonakala ngokungakhethiyo, kwindlu ngenombolo 54 kwaye babheje ukuba angenza ipropathi eyona dilesi ekuthethwa ngayo eLondon. Umhlobo wakhe wamkhwelisa ebhejini kwaye ibheji yayilungiselelwe igwini elinye.

Ubomi 
bokuqala 
uHook wazalelwa eCharlotte Street, eBedford Square, eLondon. Uyise, uJames Hook (1746–1827), wayengumqambi; umkhuluwa wakhe, okwabizwa ngokuba nguJames Hook (1771–1828), waba yiDini yaseWorcester. 

Wachitha unyaka kwisikolo saseHarrow waza emva koko waphumelela imatriki kwiYunivesithi yaseOxford. Uyise wayonwabile ekuboniseni izipho zenkwenkwe zomculo kunye ne-metrical, kwaye i-precocious Theodore yaba sisilwanyana segumbi eliluhlaza. Xa wayeneminyaka eyi-16, edibene noyise, wafumana impumelelo emangalisayo kunye ne-The Soldier's Return, i-comic opera, kwaye ilandele uthotho lwamalinge athandwayo noJohn Liston kunye noCharles Mathews, kuquka noTekeli. 

UHook ke waba ngumdlali odlalayo kunye neqhula elisebenzayo elaziwa kakhulu ngeBerners Street hoax ngo-1810 apho walungiselela intaphane yabarhwebi, kunye nezinto eziphawulekayo ezifana neNkosi uSodolophu waseLondon, iRhuluneli yeBhanki yaseNgilani, uSihlalo we-East India Company kunye noDuke waseGloucester ukutyelela uNksk. iveki. 

Enye i-prank ephawulekayo yeHook yadlalwa kumdlali we-Robert ("Romeo") uCoates, kwiminyaka embalwa kamva. I-Prince Regent ngelo xesha yayibambe "udumo lobungangamsha obugqwesileyo" eCarlton House, kunye noCoates "kuzuko lwakhe- ebulala uShakespeare eHaymarket"; belinganisa umbhalo weChamberlain, uHook wavelisa itikiti "eyalela ubukho bukaSignor Romeo" kwaye, enxibe i-livery ebomvu ecikizekileyo, wazisa esi simemo simnandi kodwa singeyonyani kuCoates ngokobuqu. UHook walinda ukuze abukele uCoates ekhwela umsitho kwinqwelo yakhe, "ethobela yonke into yakhe entle, ngekrele elinqatyisiweyo ledayimani kunye nomoya kaLouis le Grand". UCoates wamenywa ukuba angene, kodwa ekuvavanyeni itikiti "waxelelwa ngokuchubekileyo ukuba kwenzeke impazamo", wajika. Emva kokuba eyindulule inqwelo yakhe, kwanyanzeleka ukuba alinde kwisikhululo senqwelo saseHackney. INkosana, ibandakanyeka ngokungaziyo kwi-prank kaHook, yazisola ngokuba uCoates ejikiwe, embona ukuba "yindoda ekhohlakeleyo", kwaye ephawula ukuba "ubukho bakhe bunokuzothusa uninzi lweendwendwe, kwaye akukho mntu unokwenzakalisa." INkosana yathumela unobhala wayo kusuku olulandelayo, wamema uCoates ukuba eze kujonga “amalungiselelo nezihombiso” ezazisahleli; UCoates wahamba ngokufanelekileyo. Umsebenzi
Wahlala eSt Mary Hall, kwiYunivesithi yaseOxford, wemka emva kwamaxesha amabini ukuze aqhubeke nobomi bakhe bangaphambili. Isipho sakhe sokuphucula iingoma sithande uMlawuli oyiNkosana ukuba enze isibhengezo sokuba kukho into emayenzelwe uHook, owaqeshwa njengomgcini-mali jikelele kunye nonondyebo waseMauritius ngomvuzo we-£ 2,000 ngonyaka (i-£ 142,325; i-US $ 194,771 ngokwemiqathango ye-2021). Wayebubomi kunye nomphefumlo wesiqithi ukusuka ekufikeni kwakhe ngo-Okthobha 1813, kodwa intsilelo enkulu yafunyanwa kwi-akhawunti ye-treasury ngo-1817, kwaye wabanjwa waza waziswa eNgilani ngetyala lolwaphulo-mthetho. Isixa esimalunga ne-£12,000 (£1,054,500; US$1.44 yezigidi ngokwemiqathango ye-2021) yatsalwa ngusekela-gosa, kwaye iHook yabanjwa uxanduva.

Ngexesha lokuphononongwa kwebhodi yophicotho, wayehlala ngokufihlakeleyo kwaye wazigcina ngokubhalela amaphephancwadi kunye namaphephandaba. Ngelixa wayevalelwe kwindlu yesiponji ukusuka kowe-1823 ukuya kowe-1825, wabhala imiqulu elithoba yamabali emva koko aqokelelwa phantsi komxholo othi IiNtetho neZinto (1824–1828). Ekuqaleni kwee-1820s, wanceda imvumi uMichael Kelly ukuba aqulunqe i-Reminiscences yakhe, equka iinkcukacha zokusebenza noMozart. Kwiminyaka engama-23 eseleyo yobomi bakhe, wagalela imiqulu engama-38 ngaphandle kwamanqaku, ama-squibs neziketshi. Iinoveli zakhe zinemihlathi ethe gqolo yokubalisa ngorhatyazo kunye nomfanekiso ocacileyo. Baquka uMaxwell (1830), umfanekiso womhlobo wakhe uMfundisi E. Cannon; Uthando nekratshi (1833); i-autobiographical uGilbert Gurney (1835) kunye noGurney Married (1838); UJack Brag (1837) kunye noPeregrine Bunce (1842). Elona khadi leposi lidala ehlabathini lathunyelwa eHook ngo-1840, [1] liphethe isitampu sikaPenny Black. I-Hook mhlawumbi idale kwaye yathumela ikhadi kuye njengehlaya elisebenzayo kwinkonzo yeposi, ekubeni umfanekiso ufana ne-caricature yabasebenzi beposi. Kwi-2002, ikhadi leposi lithengiswa ngerekhodi ye-£ 31,750.

IWestminster 

Westminster yeyona ndawo iphambili yokuhlala kwiSixeko saseWestminster kuMbindi weLondon, eNgilani. Isuka kuMlambo iThames ukuya kwisitalato iOxford kwaye ineendawo ezininzi ezidumileyo, kubandakanya iBhotwe laseWestminster, iBuckingham Palace, iWestminster Abbey, iWestminster Cathedral, iTrafalgar Square kunye noninzi lweziko lenkcubeko laseWest End kubandakanya indawo yokuzonwabisa yaseWest End theatre. 

Igama (IsiNgesi sakudala: Westmynstre) [1] lisuka kwinkcazo engacwangciswanga yecawe ye-abbey kunye neyobukhosi obukhethekileyo beSt Peter's (Westminster Abbey), kwintshona yesiXeko saseLondon (kude kube kuHlaziyo lwamaNgesi kukwakho ne-Eastminster abbey, kwelinye icala leSixeko saseLondon, kwi-East End yaseLondon). Imvelaphi ye-abbey isukela phakathi kwe-7th kunye ne-10th inkulungwane, kodwa yaphakama ukuya kwisikhundla esiphakamileyo xa yaphinda yakhiwa ngu-Edward uMvuma ngenkulungwane ye-11. Ngophuhliso lwebhotwe elidala ecaleni kwe-abbey, iWestminster ibilikhaya likarhulumente waseNgilani ukusukela malunga ne-1200, kwaye ukusukela ngo-1707 uRhulumente wase-United Kingdom. Ngowe-1539, yaba sisixeko. 

IWestminster ihlala isetyenziswa njengegama elithetha ukubhekisa kwiPalamente yase-United Kingdom, ehlala kwiBhotwe laseWestminster. 

IJografi 
yoBume beJografi 
ISixeko kunye neNkululeko yaseWestminster kunye namanye amacandelo olawulo eWestminster (ngaphandle kwesiXeko sanamhlanje esibanzi saseWestminster, iLondon Borough eyadalwa ngo-1965) yandiswa ukusuka kuMlambo iThames ukuya kwindlela endala yaseRoma ukusuka kwiSixeko ukuya kwintshona yeNgilani, ngoku ebizwa ngokuba yiOxford Street. 




Westminster ngaphambi kokufudukela ezidolophini. Indlela yaseRoma (iSitrato sanamhlanje iOxford) iboniswe phezulu ihamba ngasentshona. 
Isiqithi saseThorney silele phakathi kweengalo zoMlambo iTyburn wangaphambili ekudibaneni kwawo nomlambo iThames, ngelixa umda osentshona kunye neChelsea wasekwa nguMlambo iWestbourne owalahleka ngendlela efanayo. Umgca womlambo usabumba (nohlaziyo oluncinci kakhulu) imida yommandla wangoku kunye neRoyal Borough yaseKensington kunye neChelsea. 


Amarhamente kunye neNdawo zeSixeko kunye neNkululeko yaseWestminster. Umzantsi weWestbourne wenze inxenye yomda osentshona, kunye nesitalato iOxford esisemantla. 
Emantla, kude nomlambo, iWestminster ibandakanya umhlaba kumacala omabini eWestbourne,ngakumbi iKnightsbridge (kubandakanywa iindawo zeHyde Park kwintshona yechibi iSerpentine (ekuqaleni yayiyilwe ngokugrumba umlambo) kunye noninzi lweeGadi zaseKensington).


I-Westminster ibandakanya izithilana zaseSoho, eSt James, eMayfair, eCovent Garden (ekwabelwana ngayo neCamden engummelwane), iPimlico, iVictoria, iBelgravia kunye neKnightsbridge (ekwabelwana ngayo neKensington engummelwane). 

IsiXeko sangaphambili saseWestminster sadityaniswa neendawo ezingabamelwane zasePaddington kunye neMarylebone ngo-1965 ukuze zenze indawo enkulu yale mihla. Le mimandla ingabamelwane (ngaphandle kwendawo encinci yasePaddington kwinxalenye yeGadi yaseKensington), imi emantla eSitalato iOxford kunye nokuqhubekeka kwayo ngasentshona, iBayswater Road.

Omnye wabalali yayiligqwetha eliselula elalibizwa ngokuba ngu-Edward Blackmore, owachukumiseka ngulo mncinci waza wamqesha njengegqwetha e-Ellisn 1 Blackmore (umvuzo ophindwe kabini wowoyikwa kukaDickens, nowawusaziwayo, umsebenzi womzimveliso omnyama). 

KwakuseSitrato saseBerners apho, eseyinkwenkwana, uDickens wabona umfazi obhadulayo, apho umlinganiswa onguNkosazana Havisham ovela kuLindiso olukhulu wayesekelwe phezu kwakhe. UDickens wamchaza "njengesidalwa esidala esizidlayo, esibandayo kwaye esisesikweni" "esinxibe mhlophe ngokupheleleyo kunye nentsimbi emhlophe eyoyikisayo ejikeleze intloko kunye nobuso ngaphakathi kwebhonethi yakhe emhlophe." Wongeze ngelithi "waphambana ngezizathu zakhe yedwa - ngaphandle kwamathandabuzo ngenxa yokuba isityebi seQuaker sasingafuni ukumtshata. Yilokhwe yakhe yomtshakazi le. Uhlala enyuka apha… sibona kwinyathelo lakhe elithambileyo kunye neliso elilotywayo ukuba ujonge ukumkhokelela kubomi obubukhali." Oku kwakukwisincoko sakhe esithi "Apho Siyeke Ukukhula" esapapashwa kwi-Household Words ngomhla we-1 kaJanuwari 1853. 

Kamva ebomini, kwi-31 Berners Street, uDickens wafaka umthandi wakhe omncinci oyimfihlo, umdlali we-actress u-Ellen Lawless Ternan (obizwa ngokuba nguNelly), owayeneminyaka eyi-19 iminyaka engama-27 emncinci kuye, kunye nentombi yakhe endala. KwakungoSeptemba 1858 xa wangena ephelekwa ngunina uFrancis, owayengumdlali weqonga, kunye noodade ababini, uMaria, omnye umdlali weqonga, noFanny, imvumi. Emva kwenyanga nje uEllen noMaria bamxelela ukuba babengcungcuthekiswa lipolisa, uDickens awayerhanela ukuba lalinyotywe yindoda eyayinomdla ngokwesini kuwo omabini okanye omabini. Wakhalazela le “ndlela ingaqhelekanga, kwaye iyingozi nengafunekiyo kwipolisa” awayecinga ukuba kufuneka igxothwe. Kodwa ukoyika ukubhengezwa kuye kwamthintela ekusukeleni. Mhlawumbi yiloo nto ke bafuduka bonke ngoMatshi 1859 ukuya eAmpthill Square. U-Ellen wayenomkhwa wokugqoka iigeraniums ezibomvu kunye ne-heather emhlophe ezinweleni zakhe. Wacengwa ukuba ayeke ukusebenza nguDickens ngo-Agasti 1859, kwaye wafumana iintlawulo rhoqo kwiakhawunti yakhe yaseCoutts Bank. Ekufeni kwakhe eneminyaka engama-75 ubudala, wangcwatyelwa eSouthsea, kufutshane nalapho uDickens wazalelwa khona.

IBhanki yaseNgilani yibhanki esembindini yase-United Kingdom kunye nemodeli apho uninzi lweebhanki ezingoovimba bale mihla zisekwe kuyo. Yasekwa ngo-1694 ukuba isebenze njengomgcini-bhanki kuRhulumente wamaNgesi kunye nomphathi wamatyala, kwaye isengomnye wabagcini bebhanki kurhulumente wase-United Kingdom, yibhanki enguvimba enkulu yesibini kwihlabathi.

Le bhanki yayiyeyabucala ngabaninimasheya ukususela kwisiseko sayo ngo-1694 de yaphunyezwa ngo-1946 yinkonzo yase-Attlee.[4] Ngo-1998 yaba ngumbutho karhulumente ozimeleyo, ophethwe ngokupheleleyo nguNondyebo egameni likarhulumente, [5] kunye nesigunyaziso sokuxhasa imigaqo-nkqubo yezoqoqosho yorhulumente welo xesha, [6] kodwa ukuzimela geqe ekugcineni uzinzo lwamaxabiso.[7] Ngenkulungwane yama-21 ibhanki ithathe uxanduva olongezelelekileyo lokugcina nokubeka iliso uzinzo lwezezimali e-UK, kwaye iya isanda ukusebenza njengomlawuli osemthethweni. 

Ikomkhulu lebhanki belikwisithili semali saseLondon, isiXeko saseLondon, ukusukela ngo-1694, nakwiSitalato i-Threadneedle ukusukela ngo-1734. Ngamanye amaxesha yaziwa ngokuba yi-"Old Lady of Threadneedle Street", igama elithathwe kwikhathuni ye-satirical nguJames Gillray ngo-1797. Indlela yokuhlangana ngaphandle yaziwa ngokuba yi-Bank Junction. 

Ibhanki, phakathi kwezinye izinto, ingumlondolozi koovimba begolide abasemthethweni baseUnited Kingdom (kunye nabo bamalunga nama-30 amanye amazwe).[10] Ukusukela ngo-Epreli ka-2016, ibhanki ibambe malunga ne-5,134 yeetoni (iitoni ezi-5,659) zegolide, ezixabisa i-£ 141 yebhiliyoni. Olu qikelelo lucebisa ukuba ivault inokubamba ukuya kutsho kwi-3% yeetoni zegolide ezili-171,300 zombiwa kwimbali yoluntu. 

Imisebenzi 
Ngokwe-strapline yayo, eyona njongo yebhanki 'kukukhuthaza okulungileyo kwabantu base-United Kingdom ngokugcina uzinzo lwemali kunye nezezimali'. Oku kuphunyezwa ngeendlela ezahlukeneyo: 

Uzinzo lwemali 
Amaxabiso azinzileyo kunye neendlela ezikhuselekileyo zentlawulo zezona nqobo ziphambili zokumisela uzinzo lwemali. 

Amaxabiso azinzileyo 
Amaxabiso azinzileyo agcinwa ngokuzama ukuqinisekisa ukuba ukonyuka kwamaxabiso kuyahlangabezana nommiselo kaRhulumente wokunyuka kwamaxabiso. Ibhanki ijolise ekuhlangabezaneni nale njongo ngokulungelelanisa isiseko senzala (eyaziwa ngokuba yizinga lebhanki), eligqitywe yiKomiti yoMgaqo-nkqubo weMali webhanki (MPC). (I-MPC iye yanikezela uxanduva lokulawula umgaqo-nkqubo wezemali; i-HM Treasury inamagunya agciniweyo okunika imiyalelo kwikomiti "ukuba ifunwa ngokwentando yoluntu kunye neemeko zoqoqosho ezigqithisileyo", kodwa iPalamente kufuneka ivume loo myalelo kwiintsuku ezingama-28.) 

Ukususela ngo-2024 i-inflation target yi-2%; ukuba le thagethi iphosiwe iRhuluneli kufuneka ibhale ileta evulekileyo kwiNgqonyela ye-Exchequer echaza imeko kunye nokucebisa izilungiso.[15] Ngaphandle kokumisela isiseko senzala, esona sixhobo sisetyenziswa yibhanki kulo mba kukuphunguka kobungakanani. 

Iifomu ezikhuselekileyo zentlawulo
Ibhanki ine-monopoly kwimiba yemali ebhankini eNgilani naseWales kwaye ilawula ukukhutshwa kweebhanki kwiibhanki zorhwebo eScotland naseMntla Ireland. (Scottish kunye Northern Irish banks bagcina ilungelo lokukhupha iimali zabo, kodwa kufuneka baxhaswe enye-nganye kunye iidiphozithi ebhankini, ngaphandle kwezigidi ezimbalwa zeeponti ezimele ixabiso amanqaku ababenawo kujikelezo 1845.) 

Ukongeza ibhanki yongamela ezinye iinkqubo zokuhlawula, esebenza njenge-arhente yokuhlalisa kunye neenkqubo zokuhlalisa i-ChaPS yexesha elililo. [18] Ngo-2024 ibhanki yayizinzile malunga ne-500 yeebhiliyoni zeerandi zeentlawulo phakathi kweebhanki suku ngalunye. 

Uzinzo lwezemali 
Ukugcina uzinzo lwemali kubandakanya ukukhusela abagcini-mali base-UK, abatyali-mali kunye nababoleki ngokuchasene nezoyikiso kwinkqubo yezemali xa iyonke.[8] Izoyikiso zibonwa yibhanki kunye nemisebenzi yobuntlola kwimarike, kwaye kujongwana nayo ngokwemali neminye imisebenzi (ekhaya nakwamanye amazwe). Uninzi lwezi zikhuselo zafakwa emva kwengxaki yezemali ka-2008: 

Ummiselo 
Ngo-2011 ibhanki ye-Prudential Regulation Authority yasekwa ukuze ilawule kwaye ibeke iliso kuzo zonke iibhanki ezinkulu, imibutho yokwakha, imibutho yamatyala, ii-inshurensi kunye neefemu zotyalo-mali e-UK ('ulawulo oluncinci').[19] Ibhanki ikwanendima esemthethweni yokulawula ngokunxulumene neziseko zophuhliso lwemalike yemali. 

Ulawulo lomngcipheko 
Kwangaxeshanye, iKomiti yeFinancial Policy yebhanki (FPC) yamiselwa ukuze ibone kwaye ibeke iliso kumngcipheko wenkqubo yezemali, kwaye ithathe amanyathelo afanelekileyo apho kuyimfuneko ('ulawulo oluphangaleleyo'). I-FPC ipapasha iziphumo zayo (kunye namanyathelo athathiweyo) kwiNgxelo yoZinzo lwezeMali kabini ngonyaka. 

Iinkonzo zebhanki 
Ibhanki ibonelela ngeenkonzo zebhanki kwi-wholesale kuRhulumente wase-UK (kunye nangaphezulu kwekhulu leebhanki ezingoovimba zaphesheya).[22] Ilawula i-Akhawunti ye-Exchange Equalization Account yase-UK egameni likaNondyebo we-HM kwaye igcina i-akhawunti yeNgxowa-mali eManyeneyo karhulumente.[23] Ikwalawula oovimba botshintshiselwano lwamazwe angaphandle kwaye ingumlondolozi wogcino lwegolide lwase-UK (kunye nabanye). 

Ibhanki ikwabonelela 'ngenkxaso yotywala kunye nezinye iinkonzo kwiibhanki nakwamanye amaziko emali'.[13] Iibhanki zorhwebo ngokwesiqhelo zigcina umlinganiselo wemali ebekelwe bucala kwidiphozithi kwiBhanki yaseNgilani. Ezi zovimba zebhanki zisetyenziswa ziibhanki ukuhlawula omnye nomnye;[25] (ngesi sizathu iBhanki yaseNgilani ngamanye amaxesha ibizwa ngokuba 'yibhanki yebhanki').[26] Kwiimeko ezingaqhelekanga, iBhanki isenokuthi ibe ngumbolekisi wemali yokugqibela ngokuthi ibolekwe ngetyala xa lingekho elinye iziko.

Njengomlawuli kunye nebhanki ephakathi, iBhanki yaseNgilani ayizange ibonelele ngeenkonzo zebhanki zabathengi iminyaka emininzi, kodwa isalawula ezinye iinkonzo ezijongene noluntu (ezifana nokutshintshiselana ngamanqaku ebhanki afakwe endaweni).[27] Kuze kube yi-2017, abasebenzi beBhanki babenelungelo lokuvula ii-akhawunti zangoku ngokuthe ngqo kwiBhanki yaseNgilani kwaye banikwa ikhowudi yohlobo olulodwa lwe-10-00-00. 

ISigqibo 
Phantsi kwemiqathango yoMthetho wokuBhankisha ka-2009, ibhanki yi-UK's Resolution Authority kuyo nayiphi na ibhanki okanye umbutho wesakhiwo obonwa 'mkhulu kakhulu ukuba ungasilela'; ngoko ke ixhotyisiwe ukuba isebenze kwimeko yokusilela kwebhanki 'ukukhusela iinkonzo zezemali ezibalulekileyo zase-UK kunye nokuzinza kwezemali'. 

Iinkonzo zembali kunye noxanduva 
Phakathi kuka-1715 no-1998, iBhanki yaseNgilani yayilawula i-Government Stocks (eyayiyimali eninzi yetyala lesizwe): ibhanki yayinoxanduva lokukhupha izitokhwe kubaninimasheya, ukuhlawula izabelo kunye nokugcina irejista yokudluliselwa; iphinde yathatha ulawulo lwemali ye-Exchequer noxanduva lokukhupha amatyala kaNondyebo kwibhanki ngo-2000. [30] I-Computershare ithathe indawo njengombhalisi kwiibhondi zikaRhulumente wase-UK (ii-gilt-edged securities okanye 'gilts') ukusuka ebhankini ekupheleni kuka-2004. Ibhanki, nangona kunjalo, iyaqhubeka isebenza njenge-arhente yokuhlawula i-Ofisi yoLawulo lweTyala kunye nomgcini wezibambiso zayo. 

Ukusukela ekusekweni kwayo ngo-1694, ibhanki ibonelele ngenkonzo yebhanki yokuthengisa kuRhulumente; nangona kunjalo ngo-2008 yagqiba ekubeni irhoxe ekunikezeni ezi nkonzo, ngoku zibonelelwa ngoluhlu lwamanye amaziko emali kwaye alawulwa yiNkonzo yeBhanki kaRhulumente. 

Kuze kube ngo-2016, ibhanki ibonelele ngeenkonzo zebhanki zobuqu njengelungelo kubasebenzi.[28] Ngaphambili, ibhanki ibigcine iiakhawunti zabucala kunye nezorhwebo kuzo zonke iintlobo zabathengi, kubandakanywa abantu, amashishini amancinci kunye nemibutho yoluntu; kodwa utshintsho lomgaqo-nkqubo olandela iMfazwe yeHlabathi yokuQala yabona ibhanki isanda ukurhoxa kolu hlobo lweshishini ukuze igxininise ngakumbi kwindima yayo yebhanki ephakathi.

Oku kuya kuba nesiphumo sokunyusa amaxabiso ee-asethi zeebhondi ezithengiweyo, ngokwenjenjalo kuthotywa izivuno kunye nokunciphisa inzala yexesha elide. Injongo yalo mgaqo-nkqubo ekuqaleni ibikukunciphisa imiqobo yokuhlawula amatyala kwinkqubo ye-sterling reserves kodwa yaguquka yaba ngumgaqo-nkqubo obanzi wokubonelela ngovuselelo lwezoqoqosho. 

I-QE yenziwe ngezigaba ezintandathu phakathi kwe-2009 kunye ne-2020. Kwincopho yayo kwi-2020, iphothifoliyo iphelele i-£ 895 yezigidigidi, equka i-£ 875 yeebhiliyoni zebhondi zikaRhulumente wase-UK kunye ne-£ 20 yeebhiliyoni ze-high-grade bonds zorhwebo. 

NgoFebruwari 2022, iBhanki yaseNgilani yabhengeza injongo yayo yokuqalisa ukuvala ipotfoliyo yeQE.[113] Ekuqaleni oku bekuya kuphunyezwa ngokungatshintshwa kwezabelo zeebhondi ezivuthwayo, kwaye kamva bekuya kukhawuleziswa ngokuthengiswa kwebhondi esebenzayo. 

Ngo-Agasti ka-2022, iBhanki yaseNgilani yaphinda yaphinda injongo yayo yokukhawulezisa i-QE-wind-down ngokuthengiswa kwebhondi esebenzayo. Lo mgaqo-nkqubo waqinisekiswa kutshintshiselwano lweeleta phakathi kweBhanki yaseNgilani kunye neChancellor yase-UK ye-Exchequer ngoSeptemba 2022. [114] Phakathi kukaFebruwari 2022 kunye noSeptemba 2022, iyonke i-£ 37.1bn yeebhondi zikarhulumente zikhulile, zinciphisa isitokhwe esibalaseleyo ukusuka kwi-£ 875.0bn ekupheleni kwe-2021 ukuya kwi-837.9bn. Ukongezelela, isamba se-£ 1.1bn yeebhondi zenkampani zikhulile, ukunciphisa isitokhwe kwi-£ 20.0bn ukuya kwi-£ 18.9bn, kunye nokuthengiswa kwempahla eseleyo ecetywayo ukuqala ngo-27 Septemba.

Inqaku eliphambili: I-Bank of England ikhupha amanqaku 

Inqaku le-£1 lomhla ka-1797 (yaye yaphawulwa njengehlawulwe ngo-1928) 
Ibhanki iye yakhupha imali engamaphepha ukususela ngo-1694. Amanqaku ayebhalwe ngesandla ekuqaleni; nangona zashicilelwa ngokuyinxenye ukususela ngowe-1725 ukuya phambili, oonomali kwakusafuneka batyobele inqaku ngalinye baze babhatalwe komnye umntu. Amanqaku aprintwa ngokupheleleyo ukususela ngo-1855. Ukuza kuthi ga ngo-1928 onke amanqaku ayeyi-"White Notes", eprintwe ngombala omnyama kunye ne-reverse engenanto. Ngeenkulungwane ze-18 kunye ne-19, iiNqaku eziMhlophe zikhutshwe kwi-£ 1 kunye ne-£ 2 yeenkonzo. Ngexesha le-20th inkulungwane, amaNqaku aMhlophe akhutshwe kwiinkonzo phakathi kwe-5 kunye ne-£ 1000. Kude kwaphunyezwa uMthetho woMgangatho weGolide wowe-1925 ibhanki yayibophelelekile ukuba ihlawule ngokwemfuno ixabiso lenowuthi kwingqekembe yegolide kumphathi wayo. 

Ngo-1724 ibhanki yangena kwikhontrakthi kunye noHenry Portal waseWhitchurch, eHamphire ukubonelela ngephepha elikumgangatho ophezulu wokushicilela imali engamaphepha.[115] Ushicilelo ngokwalo lwenziwa ziifemu zabucala zoshicilelo; iipleyiti zobhedu zazigcinwa kwivault, kwaye zikhatshwa ngexesha labo kumshicileli ngunobhala webhanki (owayeya kubhala inani leekopi ezenziweyo); xa sele yomile ziya kusiwa ebhankini. Umsebenzi wokushicilela wangeniswa kwizakhiwo zebhanki (nangona wawuphantsi kwesivumelwano sabucala) ngowe-1791; ngowe-1808 yaziswa ngokupheleleyo endlwini.


Ibhanki ye-One Guinea, eyakhutshwa yiGloucester Old Bank ngo-1813 
De kwaphakathi kwinkulungwane ye-19, iibhanki zorhwebo zazivunyelwe ukuba zikhuphe imali yazo, kwaye amanqaku akhutshwe ziinkampani zebhanki zephondo ayedla ngokujikeleza.[116] Umthetho weBhanki Charter Act 1844 uqale inkqubo yokuthintela umba wamanqaku ebhankini; Iibhanki ezintsha azizange zivunyelwe ukuba zikhuphe imali yazo, kwaye iibhanki ezikhupha amanqaku azizange zivunyelwe ukuba zandise umba wazo. Njengoko iinkampani zebhanki zephondo zadityaniswa zenza iibhanki ezinkulu, zaphulukana nelungelo lazo lokukhupha amanqaku, yaye imali yabucala yeNgesi yabhanga ekugqibeleni yanyamalala, yashiya ibhanki inolawulo olupheleleyo lwemiba yamanqaku eNgilani naseWales. Ibhanki yokugqibela yabucala ukukhupha imali yayo eNgilani naseWales yayinguFox, Fowler kunye neNkampani ngo-1921. [117] Nangona kunjalo, imida yoMthetho we-1844 ichaphazele kuphela iibhanki eNgilani naseWales, kwaye namhlanje iibhanki ezintathu zorhwebo eScotland kunye nezintathu zaseNorthern Ireland ziyaqhubeka nokukhupha iibhanki zazo, ezilawulwa yibhanki. 


Inqaku le-Treasury leeshilling ezilishumi (eyaqala ngo-1914), latyikitywa nguJohn Bradbury 
Ekuqaleni kweMfazwe Yehlabathi yokuqala, uMthetho weMali kunye neBhanki yamanqaku ka-1914 wapasiswa, owanika amagunya exeshana kuNondyebo we-HM wokukhupha imali yebhanki kumaxabiso e-£1 kunye ne-10/- (iisheleni ezilishumi). Amanqaku kaNondyebo ayenesimo esipheleleyo sethenda esisemthethweni kwaye ayengatshintshwa abe yigolide ngebhanki; bathathe indawo yengqekembe yegolide ejikelezayo ukuthintela ukubaleka kwi-sterling kunye nokwenza ukuba kuthengwe izinto ezikrwada zokuvelisa izixhobo. La manqaku abonisa umfanekiso kaKumkani uGeorge V (amanqaku eBhanki yaseNgilani ayizange iqalise ukubonisa umfanekiso wenkosi de kube yi-1960). Amagama kwinqaku ngalinye ayesithi: 

UNITED KINGDOM OF GREAT BRITAIN AND IRELAND – Currency notes are Legal Tender of Legal payment of any amount – Ikhutshwe ngabaKomishinala beNkosi beOfisi kaNondyebo phantsi koGunyaziso loMthetho wePalamente 

Amanqaku kaNondyebo akhutshwa de kwangowe-1928 xa i-Curency and Bank8 Notes powering Act 192 bank8s Act 192 IBhanki yaseNgilani yakhupha amanqaku eesheleni ezilishumi kunye neponti enye okokuqala ngomhla wama-22 kuNovemba 1928. 


Inqaku elithi 'Operation Bernhard' £ 50, elenziwe ngamabanjwa kwinkampu yoxinaniso yaseSachsenhausen 
Ngexesha leMfazwe yesibini yeHlabathi, i-Operation yaseJamani uBernhard yazama ukukhohlisa amahlelo phakathi kwe-£ 5 kunye ne-£ 50, ukuvelisa i-500,000 yemali kwi-parachu yenyanga nganye kwi-3 ye-parachu yase-UK. umzamo wokudodobalisa uqoqosho lwaseBritane, kodwa kwafunyanwa kuluncedo ngakumbi ukusebenzisa amanqaku ukuhlawula iiarhente zaseJamani ezisebenza kulo lonke elaseYurophu. Nangona uninzi lwawela kwizandla zama-Allied ekupheleni kwemfazwe, ubuqhetseba buhlala bubonakala iminyaka emva koko, nto leyo eyakhokelela ekubeni amahlelo ebhanki angaphezulu kwe-£5 asuswe ekujikelezweni. 

Ngo-2006, ngaphezulu kwe-£53 yezigidi zeemali zemali zebhanki zabiwa kwindawo yokugcina imali eTonbridge, eKent.

Ngowe-1917 ibhanki yayifudusele umsebenzi wayo wokushicilela kwiSt Luke’s Printing Works, eyayisakuba sisibhedlele; ngo-1958 yafudukela eDebden. Iimali zebhanki zala maxesha ziprintwa ngesivumelwano neDe La Rue Currency eLoughton, Essex.

IBhanki yaseNgilani, eKing Street, eLeeds: esinye sezakhiwo ezitsha zesebe ezinogonyamelo ezavulwa ekuqaleni kweminyaka yee-1970. 
Kwiminyaka emininzi yenkulungwane yeshumi elinesithoba elinesithoba ibhanki yayinamasebe amaninzi eLondon nakwezinye izixeko zaseNgilani. 

Amasebe okuqala avulwa ngo-1826 (ngenkuthazo ebonelelwe ngokupasiswa koMthetho weeBhanki zeLizwe, owathi okokuqala wavumela ukusekwa kweebhanki ezidibeneyo ngaphandle kweLondon).[75] Ibhanki ijonge ukuba uthungelwano olutsha lwamasebe luza 'kwandisa ukusasazwa kweeNkcazo zeBhanki, lunike ibhanki ulawulo olupheleleyo ngakumbi kulo lonke ujikelezo lwamaphepha, nokukhusela ibhanki kukhuphiswano lweeNkampani zebhanki ezinkulu'.[121] Isebe ngalinye lalisonganyelwa yi-Agent (umntu 'onolwazi lwezorhwebo, onamava asekuhlaleni').[75] Ngowe-1829 kwakukho amasebe alishumi elinanye asebenza (alishumi eNgilani nelinye eWales). Amanye amasebe angenangeniso kangako ayenexesha elifutshane, kodwa amanye aqhubeka esebenza kwaya kutsho ngoo-1990. 

Ngowe-1997 amasebe amahlanu okugqibela aseleyo avalwa; Ii-Agent, nangona kunjalo, zagcinwa, kunye nesakhiwo see-Arhente zeNgingqi ezenziwe (kuyo yonke i-UK), ezinye zazo bezisekelwe kwizakhiwo zesebe zangaphambili.
